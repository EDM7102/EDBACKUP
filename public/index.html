Game Description

F1 Racing Simulation is a dice-driven lap-by-lap F1 simulation. The special feature: Each driver does not race on a traditional track, but on their own individual map on your table! Each driver has five fixed and one flexible driving attribute. These are activated depending on the track and race progress and trigger unpredictable events.

During the race, drivers occasionally have to choose between two options ‚Äì for example at the start, during pit stops or overtaking maneuvers. These decisions are rare but can be strategically crucial. However, you can also choose not to intervene and simply enjoy the race as a spectator.

Game duration: Qualifying and race preparation take about 10 minutes, the race itself about 40 minutes. For your first game, you should plan double the time.

Contents: The game includes all drivers and tracks of the 2024 season. With seven different winners and four winning teams, the starting field experienced a new era of competition. Please do not purchase our simulation if you are looking for a game where you control a car or influence the outcome through strategic decisions.
